About

We are a small, old fashioned, two chair barbershop. We cherish our tradition and our workmanship, and it shows in every cut and shave that we do. We offer a range of services from straight razor shaves to comb overs and simple designs. We focus on quality and not quantity, that is why we dedicate time to each hair service we perform. And walk-ins are always welcome.
